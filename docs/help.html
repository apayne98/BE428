Blood Pressure Monitor Instructions

1. Open PregnancyBuddy

Remove the top of the device (with the buttons and screen) from the base. The lid of PregnancyBuddy contains the blood pressure monitor, while the bottom of the device holds the blood pressure cuff, urine test strips, and measuring tape.

2. Place lid on flat surface

In order for an accurate blood pressure reading, your PregnancyBuddy must be placed on a flat surface to ensure all components inside operate correctly.

3. Remove cuff from the device base and place cuff on arm

Remove the blood pressure cuff from your PregnancyBuddy’s bottom. Place the cuff on your upper arm roughly one inch above the elbow. The cuff should wrap around your arm 1.5 - 2 times, and should be tight enough that the cuff does not slide up or down your arm.

DO NOT tighten cuff to the point where the cuff is uncomfortable or cutting off circulation

SEE CONTRAINDICATIONS before placing cuff on arm

4. Connect cuff to lid

Connect the blood pressure cuff to the hose port in the blood pressure monitor located on the side of the device lid. Ensure a tight connection before proceeding.

MAKE SURE to place the device lid back on a flat surface before proceeding

5. Place arm on flat surface

Place your arm on a flat surface, such that the blood pressure cuff is roughly at heart level. Ensure both feet are firmly on the ground and that you are in a comfortable position you maintain for the duration of the blood pressure measurement.

6. Turn on PregnancyBuddy

Power on the blood pressure monitor by pressing the red “Power” button located on the top of the device lid. Wait for the screen to load before proceeding.

7. Take blood pressure measurement
a. Press the green start button

Press the green “Start” button to begin taking your blood pressure. The cuff will start to inflate. Make sure your arm is relaxed during the measurement, and try to sit still and refrain from talking for the duration of the measurement.

REMOVE CUFF if the pressure is painful or too uncomfortable

b. Wait 2 - 3 minutes

Wait 2-3 minutes for the blood pressure cuff to slowly deflate. Your blood pressure will appear on the device screen when the measurement is complete.

8. Wait for confirmation that measurement was sent to website

Wait for the confirmation message on the device screen to ensure that your measurement was successfully sent to the website and recorded

PLEASE WAIT up to 5 minutes for confirmation
